# Tornado Survival Game

A web-based interactive game where player survive a tornado by navigating a character through increasing levels of difficulty, avoiding obstacles, and collecting points. Built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.

---

## Features

- **Difficulty Levels:** Players can select **Easy, Medium, or Hard**, which affects tornado speed, obstacles, and health deduction.
- **Dynamic Gameplay:** As the difficulty increases:
  - Tornado speed increases
  - Number of obstacles increases
  - Objects (the player) move faster
- **Shields:** Temporary shields appear in each level to protect the player from tornado damage.
- **Sprint Mechanic:** Players can temporarily increase the speed of the character to escape the tornado.
- **Health System:**
  - Easy: Health starts at 10, decreases by 1 per tornado hit
  - Medium: Health decreases by 2 per tornado hit
  - Hard: Health decreases by 3 per tornado hit
- **Score Tracking:** Total score is displayed at the end of the game.
- **Responsive Design:** Works on desktop and mobile browsers.

---

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Choose a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
3. Use **arrow keys** (or touch controls) to move the character.
4. Avoid tornadoes and obstacles to survive.
5. Collect shields to protect your character temporarily.
6. Use the sprint button to move faster and escape danger.
7. Game ends when health reaches zero, and your total score is displayed.

---

## Tech Used

- **HTML5** – Structure of the game
- **CSS3** – Styling and animations
- **JavaScript** – Game logic, controls, score tracking, and difficulty mechanics

---

## Gameplay Mechanics

- **Difficulty Scaling:** Each level increases tornado speed and obstacle frequency.  
- **Health Management:** Health decreases depending on difficulty level:  
  - Easy: -1 per tornado hit  
  - Medium: -2 per tornado hit  
  - Hard: -3 per tornado hit  
- **Shields:** Temporary invincibility when collected.  
- **Sprint:** Player can move faster to dodge tornadoes.
